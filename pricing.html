Frequently asked
What counts as one image?+
Each unique image processed through the API counts as one call. Re-downloading the same result doesn't use additional calls. Preview-size results (0.25 MP) cost 0.25 credits; full resolution costs 1 credit.
Do unused images roll over?+
No, monthly allowances reset at the start of each billing period. Enterprise plans can negotiate custom rollover terms.
Can I use this without a backend or database?+
Yes — that's the whole point. The API call goes directly from your browser or app to the remove.bg API. No server-side code, no database, no sessions required. Your API key is stored only in the browser's localStorage.
Is my data private?+
Images are processed by remove.bg's API and are not stored by cutout. Remove.bg deletes uploaded images after processing. See remove.bg's privacy policy for details.
What image formats are supported?+
Input: JPG, PNG, WebP, GIF (static), BMP, TIFF — up to 22 MB and 50 megapixels. Output: PNG (transparent), JPG, WebP, ZIP. PNG is capped at 10 MP; use WebP or ZIP for larger transparent outputs.
Can I cancel anytime?+
Yes. Cancel anytime from your account page. You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period — no proration, no penalties.
